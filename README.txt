March Madness Prediction 2021

The current dataset presents the data from every D1 college team in the United States 
and the implementation of a Decision Tree regarding if a team is going to make it to playoff.

It takes into a account 5 features: 
g: Number of games played
w: Number of games won
adjoe: Adjusted Offensive Efficiency (An estimate of the offensive efficiency (points scored per 100 possessions) a team would have against the average Division I defense)
adjde: Adjusted Defensive Efficiency (An estimate of the defensive efficiency (points allowed per 100 possessions) a team would have against the average Division I offense)
barthag: Power Rating (Chance of beating an average Division I team)
